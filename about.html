ABOUT

Hello! My name is Kyle Mueller. I'm a student at BYU-Idaho studying Computer Science, and I love computers! I'm also a TA for Discrete Mathematics I & II.

I have always had a natural aptitude towards computers. Even as a boy, I would look for every opportunity to help my father with any computer related endeavors. I got really into computers in 2016, and I built my first custom computer in 2017.

In my time building PCs, I've had the chance to build over 20 custom rigs! I have a high level of expertise and knowledge when it comes to PC hardware, and I take pride in the clean aesthetic and superior cable management of my custom builds.

I'm looking forward to building a custom PC for you!
