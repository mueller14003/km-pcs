More About Me

Hello! My name is Kyle Mueller, and I love building PCs! I've been around computers ever since I was little, and building custom PCs has been a big part of my life for the past 7 years.

In the past few years, I've had the chance to build over 100 custom PCs for clients! I have a high level of knowledge and expertise when it comes to PC hardware, and I take pride in the clean aesthetic and superior cable management of my custom builds. I run rigorous stress testing and benchmarks on every PC I build to ensure the highest level of stability and performance.

I primarily build and sell PCs locally in Eastern Idaho, but if you don't live in the area, contact me and we can figure out the logistics.

I'm looking forward to building a custom PC for you!

I take pride in the quality of the PCs I build, and I hope to build one for you!
If you'd like me to configure and build a custom PC for you, contact me at kyle.mueller.custom.pcs@gmail.com
