ABOUT

Hello! My name is Kyle Mueller, and I love everything about computers. I've been around computers ever since I was little, and building custom PCs has been a big part of my life for the past 4 years.

In the past couple of years, I've had the chance to build over 60 custom PCs for clients! I have a high level of knowledge and expertise when it comes to PC hardware, and I take pride in the clean aesthetic and superior cable management of my custom builds. I run rigorous stress testing and benchmarks on every PC I build to ensure the highest level of stability and performance.

I'm looking forward to building a custom PC for you!
