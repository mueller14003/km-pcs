ABOUT

Hello! My name is Kyle Mueller. I'm a Computer Science student and TA at BYU-Idaho, and I love computers!

Ever since I was a kid, I've always had a natural aptitude towards computers. I got really into PCs in 2016, and I built my first custom PC in 2017.

In my time building PCs, I've had the chance to build over 50 custom rigs for clients! I have a high level of expertise when it comes to PC hardware, and I take pride in the clean aesthetic and superior cable management of my custom builds. I manually overclock all of the PCs I build, and I run rigorous stress testing and benchmarks to ensure the highest level of stability and performance.

I'm looking forward to building a custom PC for you!
