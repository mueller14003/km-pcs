ABOUT

Hello! My name is Kyle Mueller, and I love everything about computers. I've been around computers ever since I was little, and I've been building custom PCs for over 4 years.

In my time building PCs, I've had the chance to build over 60 custom rigs for clients! I have a high level of expertise when it comes to PC hardware, and I take pride in the clean aesthetic and superior cable management of my custom builds. I manually overclock all of the PCs I build, and I run rigorous stress testing and benchmarks to ensure the highest level of stability and performance.

I'm looking forward to building a custom PC for you!
